  LINGUAPHIX — Video folder instructions
════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Place your video files here and rename them exactly as:

  bg-video.mp4    → plays looping behind all content (background)
  bg-video.webm   → same video, WebM format (better for Firefox)

  fg-video.mp4    → plays looping where the logo was (foreground)
  fg-video.webm   → same video, WebM format (better for Firefox)

TIPS:
  • Background video: use a subtle, slow-moving clip (drone footage,
    abstract motion, dark cinematic loop). Keep file under 8 MB.
  • Foreground video: your logo animation, showreel intro, or promo
    clip. Recommended aspect ratio 16:9. Keep file under 15 MB.
  • You can use only .mp4 files — .webm is optional but recommended.
  • To adjust background opacity, find "#bg-video { opacity: 0.35; }"
    in index.html and change 0.35 (0 = invisible, 1 = full).
